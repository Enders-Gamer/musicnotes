Thanks for using MusicNotes!

Step 1: Press the 'draw staves' button or just hit the space-bar! This will draw some staves on the page.

Step 2: Use your keyboard or the buttons on screen to input notes.

Pressing 'zxcvbnm' will make a C scale from C to B in the third octave. These are very low notes and mostly in the vocal range of the average dude.

Pressing 'asdfghj' will make a C scale from C to B in the fourth octave. These are higher notes which pretty much cover the vocal range of an average woman.

Pressing 'qwertyu' will make a C scale from C to B in the fifth octave. These are high notes. A skilled soprano could sing them mostly, but better to get your hands on a flute.

Pressing '1234567' will make a C scale from C to B in the sixth octave. These are super high notes. Don't even go there.

Step 3: press delete to delete your whole song and start over.

There is no way to save other than just taking a screenshot.
